About Us

At S B Constructions, we specialize in crafting high-quality residential, commercial, and industrial spaces, with a strong focus on residential projects. From masonry and plumbing to electrical, tile laying, fabrication, and interiors, we handle every aspect of construction with precision and expertise. Whether you're building from the ground up, renovating, or enhancing interiors, we ensure a seamless and hassle-free experience. With a commitment to quality, durability, and modern craftsmanship, SB Constructions is your trusted partner in bringing your vision to life.


Meet the Founder

Mr. Hanumanthappa D B, with over 20 years of experience in construction, has led S B Constructions to success. Holding a Bachelors Degree, he specializes in Civil Engineering.
